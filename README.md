# Amazon-Prime-TV-Shows-Movies---EDA

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover content trends, audience preferences, ratings, popularity patterns, and production insights.

The analysis includes data cleaning, data wrangling, visualization, and business recommendations to support data-driven decision making.

## Business Objective

To analyze movies and TV shows data and identify factors influencing content performance, audience engagement, and ratings. The goal is to provide actionable insights that can help content providers improve content acquisition, production strategies, and viewer satisfaction.

## Key Findings

* Certain genres consistently receive higher ratings.
* TV Shows and Movies exhibit different audience engagement patterns.
* High-rated content contributes significantly to viewer satisfaction.
* Long-running series can improve audience retention.
* Content popularity and ratings provide valuable indicators for content strategy.

## Business Recommendations

* Invest in highly rated genres and content categories.
* Focus on audience-preferred content types.
* Use ratings and popularity metrics for content acquisition decisions.
* Promote content featuring well-known actors and directors.
* Maintain a balanced and diverse content portfolio.
